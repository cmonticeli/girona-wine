Cariñena

🍷 Cariñena (Carignan) Pairing Guide

Bold, rustic, and full of character, Cariñena (also known as Carignan) offers deep flavors of dark fruits, earthy spice, and smoky undertones. With high acidity and firm tannins, it pairs beautifully with rich, hearty dishes and Mediterranean flavors.

🥩 Meat

  • Grilled or roasted lamb with herbs
  • Spiced or smoked sausages
  • Braised beef or oxtail stew
  • Game meats (like venison or wild boar)
  • Barbecue ribs with smoky sauces

🧀 Cheese

  • Manchego or other aged sheep cheeses
  • Comté, Gruyère, or other firm alpine cheeses
  • Blue cheeses for a bold contrast

🥦 Vegetables & Vegetarian

  • Ratatouille and other Mediterranean vegetable stews
  • Grilled eggplant, zucchini, and bell peppers
  • Mushroom dishes with earthy seasoning

🍝 Pasta & Grains

  • Tomato-based pastas with olives or anchovies
  • Wild rice or farro with roasted vegetables
  • Lentil and chickpea stews

💡 Pro Tip

Cariñena benefits from decanting and slightly warmer serving temperatures — around 17–18°C / 62–64°F — to soften tannins and reveal its deep, rustic character.
